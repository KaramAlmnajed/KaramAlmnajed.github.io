Dialogue 1 – Morning Routine & Introductions

Karam s’est réveillé in the matin. He stretched his arms, looked at the soleil, and smiled. It was a bright day, and he felt heureux. He said bonjour to his voisin before opening the fenêtre. Then he washed his visage with cool eau, brushed his dents, and looked in the miroir. “Today,” he thought, “I will meet new people and practice my French.” He went to the cuisine to prepare his petit-déjeuner. He poured lait into his tasse and added café. He toasted some pain and spread beurre on it. While eating, he turned on the radio to listen to French music. The singer’s voix was calm and belle. After breakfast, Karam chose his chemise and pantalon. He put on his chaussures and took his sac. Outside, he saw his friend Marie and said, “Salut!” Marie smiled and replied, “Ça va?” Karam answered, “Oui, ça va bien, merci.” They walked together to the parc. Birds were singing in the arbres. A child was playing with a ballon. Marie introduced Karam to another friend, Luc. Luc said, “Enchanté,” and they shook mains. Karam asked, “What time is it?” Marie looked at her montre and said, “Il est huit heures.” They decided to walk to the boulangerie to buy more pain and some croissants. The vendeuse smiled and said, “Bonjour! Qu’est-ce que vous voulez?” Karam replied, “Deux croissants, s’il vous plaît.” After that, they sat near a fontaine and enjoyed their food. Karam felt content and ready for the rest of the day.
