Featured

I work as an independent analytics and operations consultant, helping early-stage and growth teams build systems that support better decisions.

My focus is on designing analytics frameworks, dashboards, and workflows that translate business ambiguity into structured, measurable insight. Rather than optimizing for models alone, I start from the decision being made and work backward to the data, metrics, and reporting required to support it.

Across my work, I have supported operations, sales, and strategy functions by building performance monitoring systems, CRM analytics, and operational dashboards. These systems are designed to be interpretable, scalable, and aligned with how teams actually operate.

My background combines hands-on analytics work with business development and operational execution. This allows me to bridge technical implementation with commercial and operational realities, ensuring analytics is embedded into daily workflows rather than treated as a separate function.

I am particularly interested in environments where clarity, execution, and long-term thinking matter. I value simple systems, well-defined metrics, and collaboration across functions.

Selected work includes analytics system design, operational dashboards, and data-driven growth support for early-stage companies.
